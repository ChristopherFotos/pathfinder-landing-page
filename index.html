A neat demo of the A* pathfinding algorithm

Ever wonder how apps like GoogleMaps are able to find efficient routes? The answer is pathfinding algorithms! Pathfinder is a demo of a pretty popular pathfinding algorithm called A* (pronounced 'A Star'). Keep reading to find out more!
